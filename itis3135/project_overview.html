KBT Reading Support - Project Overview

This application will serve a small tutoring business for kids with dsylexia. The purpose of this application is to serve as a portal for the owner as well as the parents of the students.

The intended users of this website are the owner and the parents, not the children.

The content of the website will include a calendar of a parent or the owners sessions for that month. Additionally, there will be options to send or view an invoice, cancel or reschedule one or all sessions, write and view session notes, email the owner or email a parent, join the zoom session, and add or remove a client. The parents and the owner will have a different dashboard.
