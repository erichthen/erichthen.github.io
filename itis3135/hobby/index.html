What is meditation?

Typical meditation does not entail sitting like a monk for hours on end in order to reach enlightenment. Meditation is hard to define because it comes in so many forms. I like to think of it as the deliberate exploration of your consciousness. Well, what is consciousness? Consciousness, to me, is the awareness of ones existence. So through meditation, you explore, observe, and can even change the ways in which your consciousness percieves both yourself and the world you exist in.

  • You can practice meditation to relax, focus, improve your health, and more
  • The practice of meditation is thousands of years old
  • Science has a lot of catching up to do, as thorough research on meditation is only recent
  • Meditation can be done in both religous and secular manners
  • Meditation can change the literal structure of your brain
